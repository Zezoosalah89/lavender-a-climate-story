About

Our solution is a video game embedded on a webpage. The game is an interactive story representing the data about Climate Change within the story events and the dialogues between characters. The story's protagonist will be a girl from the future who can communicate with the environment and feel its pain. The deuteragonist will be a scientist in the future who will help her save the planet by travelling to the past (2024) and preventing Climate Change from worsening. The story will show the causes of climate change, the dark future due to its worsening and ways to reduce its effect starting from now. The characters will explain scientific topics and the player should experience different techniques of reducing Climate Change effects.
